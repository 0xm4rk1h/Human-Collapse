# **Story**

Alone in a silent world, you open your eyes in the heart of a forgotten civilization, where nature has reclaimed its rights, engulfing the remnants of a bygone era of technology and science.

No sign of human life is visible.

You are the last human in this world. You do not know what happened, where you came from, or who you are.

# **Main Challenge**

Your mission is clear: **rebuild an entire civilization from scratch**. From a simple wooden stick to the most advanced technologies.

But the true goal lies beyond mere survival: **uncover the truth** by obtaining the **Tome of Knowledge**.

![alt text](https://i.postimg.cc/FKBxfqvr/final-quest.png)

_Once you obtain the **Tome of Knowledge**, you will receive a book containing information that will lead you to the truth. To uncover this truth, you will leave Minecraft and make use of **[Cryptography](https://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/teaching/2324/Crypto/crypto-slides.pdf)** and **[Steganography](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/381177444_A_New_Approach_of_Steganography_on_Image_Metadata/fulltext/666361c9de777205a3185d8c/A-New-Approach-of-Steganography-on-Image-Metadata.pdf)**. Good Luck_
